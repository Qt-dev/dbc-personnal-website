A fun fact : I make crepes!

There are actually many quirky things, and the more time you'll spend with me, the more you'll know about them. Even I discover some every day. But today, I chose to tell you about the fact that I can cook Crepes with... pretty much anything as long as you have some ingredients.

When I was in Korea, I used to introduce french culture to kindergartens all around the country. I had a 6 weeks program with different tasks, materials, every week. One week was "cooking week" and so I was making crepes... But let me tell you that when when you have to cook crepes for a whole school, the ingredients you have are a bottle of milk, 12 eggs, a ton of wheat flour, and the tools are a wooden spoon and a "plancha"... You have to become very creative very fast. So that's one thing about me, I became more and more able to cook crepes with what I had here and there.
